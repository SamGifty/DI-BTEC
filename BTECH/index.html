What is Climate Change?

Climate change refers to significant, long-term changes in the global climate. The global climate is the connected system of sun, earth and oceans, wind, rain and snow, forests, deserts and savannas, and everything people do.

In Ghana, climate change is already affecting weather patterns, agriculture, coastal areas, and livelihoods. Temperatures have increased by about 1Â°C over the past 50 years, and rainfall patterns have become more erratic.

Coastal Erosion

Rising sea levels are eroding Ghana's coastline at rates of up to 8 meters per year, threatening communities like Keta and Ada.

Erratic Rainfall

Rainfall patterns have become unpredictable, with longer dry spells and more intense downpours causing floods and droughts.

About Us

GreenGhana Alliance is dedicated to raising awareness and promoting sustainable practices to combat climate change in Ghana. We work with communities, organizations, and policymakers to create a greener future.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities to take meaningful action against climate change through education, advocacy, and sustainable initiatives. We believe that by working together, we can protect our environment and build a more resilient future for generations to come.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate environmentalists, educators, and community organizers committed to making a difference. We bring diverse skills and experiences to address the complex challenges of climate change in Ghana.
